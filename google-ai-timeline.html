The Innovator's Dilemma

Google invented the Transformer (2017), employed nearly all top AI talent circa 2014, had the best infrastructure (TPUs), and deployed AI at massive scale before anyone else. Yet ChatGPT caught them completely flat-footed.

Why?

  • Revenue conflict: AI answers threaten $140B search ads business
  • Legal concerns: Decades fighting publisher disintermediation perception
  • Brand trust: Google Search was trusted; chatbots were unproven
  • No market demand: Until ChatGPT, consumers didn't know they wanted this
  • Cultural barriers: Very high bar for products competing with search

The Question: Can Google disrupt themselves without destroying their profit machine?
